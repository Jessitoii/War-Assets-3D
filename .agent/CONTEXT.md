# The Soul of the Project

## Why?
To empower analysts, enthusiasts, and educators with a **fast, beautiful, and trustworthy** mobile encyclopedia of modern military platforms—delivered as a native iOS‑styled React‑Native app that works offline and leverages cutting‑edge 3‑D visualization.

## Core Philosophy
- **Transparency:** All data is public‑domain; no copyrighted material.
- **Performance‑First:** Every interaction must stay > 60 fps on iPhone 12+.
- **User‑Centric:** Minimal friction, progressive disclosure, and tactile feedback.
- **Future‑Proof:** Modular architecture so new asset categories or AR features can be added without refactoring core layers.

## Target Audience & Personas
| Persona | Motivation | Pain Points |
|---------|------------|-------------|
| **Analyst** (defense researcher) | Deep dive into specs, compare assets | Slow loading, data gaps |
| **Enthusiast** (hobbyist) | Explore 3‑D models, share screenshots | Clunky UI, missing AR |
| **Educator** (teacher) | Use as teaching aid, offline class | Internet‑dependence, complex UI |

## Vibe
- **Minimal Military Aesthetic:** Slate‑gray, muted blues, crisp lines.
- **Glassmorphism:** Subtle translucency for panels, respecting Apple HIG.
- **Responsive Gestures:** Fluid, physics‑based interactions.
- **Quiet Confidence:** No flashy ads, just data.
